PRYPCOThe Problem
2 / 6

The customer problem.

A first-time investor has the intent but not the knowledge to act confidently.

Which property is right for me?

The user sees a list of properties with ROI numbers but has no context for what's good, what's risky, or what matches their goals.

How much should I invest?

No guidance on portfolio allocation. A first-timer doesn't know if AED 500 or AED 5,000 is the right starting point for their situation.

Is this Sharia-compliant?

A significant portion of the UAE/MENA investor base requires Sharia-compliant investment options. There's no filtering or certification visible.

When should I exit?

No guidance on when to sell tokens on the marketplace or how to think about holding periods vs. liquidity needs.

Is my portfolio diversified?

Once an investor holds multiple properties, there's no AI layer telling them if they're overexposed to one area or asset type.

What are my returns vs. benchmark?

No comparison to Dubai real estate market benchmarks, rental yield averages, or alternative asset classes.

PRYPCOThe AI Approach
3 / 6

The AI approach: hybrid human + AI advisor.

Not a black box - a transparent, explainable advisor with human escalation.

01

LLM-powered conversational advisor

  • Answers investor questions in real time: 'Is this Sharia-compliant?', 'What's the exit strategy?'
  • Explains complex concepts in plain language: SPV structure, gross vs net yield, token liquidity
  • Available 24/7 - critical for international investors across time zones
  • Escalates to human advisor for high-value, complex, or sensitive queries
02

AI recommendation engine

  • Analyzes investor profile: risk tolerance, investment horizon, nationality, portfolio size
  • Ranks available properties by fit score - not just yield, but alignment to investor goals
  • Surfaces Sharia-compliant options automatically when relevant to the user profile
  • Learns from investor behavior - which properties they viewed, saved, and invested in
03

Portfolio intelligence layer

  • Tracks portfolio diversification - alerts when overexposed to one location or asset type
  • Benchmarks returns against Dubai real estate market averages and rental yield indices
  • Predicts optimal re-investment timing based on portfolio performance and market signals
  • Generates monthly plain-language portfolio summary: 'Your best performing property this month was...'
PRYPCOTrust & Compliance
4 / 6

Trust, compliance & explainability.

An AI advisor that cannot be explained cannot be trusted - especially in a regulated investment context.

Explainability

  • Every AI recommendation shows its reasoning: 'Recommended because: high yield, matches your AED 1,000-5,000 range, Dubai Marina exposure you don't yet have'
  • No black box outputs - the investor always sees why, not just what
  • Confidence level shown per recommendation: High / Medium / Needs human review

VARA compliance

  • AI advisory classified as decision-support, not financial advice - avoids MiFID-equivalent licensing issues
  • All AI recommendations reviewed by human advisors before being surfaced at high-value thresholds
  • VARA disclosure shown on every AI-generated recommendation: 'This is not financial advice'
  • Regulatory audit trail - every recommendation logged with reasoning for review

Sharia compliance

  • Properties screened for Sharia compliance by certified scholars - AI surfaces the result, not the screening
  • Filter: 'Show Sharia-compliant only' available across all property views and recommendations
  • AI never makes Sharia compliance determinations - it retrieves and displays certified status only
  • Certification partner displayed per property for transparency

Human-in-the-loop

  • AI handles 80% of queries autonomously - FAQs, property comparisons, yield calculations
  • High-value queries (AED 50,000+), complex situations, and low-confidence recommendations escalate to human
  • Human advisor sees full AI reasoning before taking over - no cold start
  • Response time SLA: AI instant, human within 4 hours UAE business hours
