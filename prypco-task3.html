PRYPCOTrust & Compliance

Built to be trusted.

Explainable, VARA-compliant, Sharia-aware, human-backed.

Explainability

Every recommendation shows its reasoning — yield match, risk fit, portfolio gap. Confidence level: High / Medium / Needs review.

VARA compliance

AI is decision-support, not financial advice. Disclosure on every recommendation. Full audit trail maintained.

Sharia compliance

Scholar-certified screening. AI surfaces the result only — never makes determinations itself.

Human-in-the-loop

AI handles 80% autonomously. AED 50K+ queries escalate to a human within 4 hours, with full AI context handed over.
