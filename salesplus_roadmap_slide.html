What's Coming Next for Sales+

Empower every agent in the field. Sales+ is an all-in-one field sales platform built to streamline order management, customer engagement, and on-ground operations — giving sales leaders real-time visibility and the insights they need to drive growth.

Long-term product roadmap · CEO Briefing · May 2026

Field Operations
Coordinate agents on the ground with real-time visit tracking, instant operational alerts, and on-site merchandising audits.
CRM
Build a CRM tailored to your specific industry, workflow, and processes for easier customer management.
Commercial Controls
Set the rules that govern how your team sells — configurable discounts, multi-tier approval workflows, and agent payout automation.
Intelligence & Growth
Turn field data into decisions — track agent performance, set targets, run market surveys, and surface insights through AI and BI dashboards.
Catalog Management
Control SKUs, pricing tiers, and product content from one place — ensuring agents always sell with accurate, up-to-date information.
Inventory Management
Stay ahead of demand, prevent stockouts, and streamline fulfillment with software tailored to how you move inventory.
Invoice Processing
Automate invoice processing and eliminate hours of manual data entry for email and paper documents.
Logistics
Optimize routes, coordinate shipments, and manage carriers with software tailored to your freight operations.
Warehouse Management
Streamline receiving, organize inventory, and manage suppliers with software designed around your warehouse layout.
Procurement
Keep on top of orders, vendors, and budgets with an app customized to how your team sources and controls spend.
Vendor Management
Track vendor performance, manage relationships, and ensure compliance with tools configured to your standards.
Knowledge Base
Equip your field teams with the knowledge, answers, and resources they need in one dedicated app.
Offline Support
Keep core operations running smoothly even when agents lose internet connection in the field.
