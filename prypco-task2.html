PRYPCOPipeline
3 / 6

Visual assets & the AI pipeline.

Three asset categories require action. The AI pipeline handles strings in D1-D5.

Visual asset categories

Replace - Images with embedded text
  • Property price banners
  • Promo cards with 'AED 500'
  • Onboarding illustrations with labels
  • Replace with Arabic version or text overlay
Mirror - Directional icons & UI
  • Back arrow (- becomes ->)
  • Property carousel: swipe direction reverses
  • Investment slider: fill starts from right
  • DO NOT mirror: camera, search, share, refresh
Audit - Brand & regulatory assets
  • VARA / DFSA / DLD logos: verify Arabic variants exist
  • App Store screenshots: recreate with Arabic UI
  • Property labels: use Arabic place names

Key risks & mitigations

HIGH
MT mistranslates financial/legal terms. Fintech glossary in Claude prompt. 100% native speaker review on D3.
MED
Directional images missed - embedded English ships in AR. D6 visual asset checklist. Automated screenshot diff on D8.
MED
Arabic font causes FOUT or increases bundle significantly. Subset Noto Sans Arabic. Preload font. Measure D8 performance baseline.
HIGH
RTL breaks EN screens for existing users. All RTL scoped to locale=ar only. Feature flag. EN users unaffected.
MED
Arabic regulatory logos unavailable. Confirm with legal/brand on D1. Use universal logo if unavailable.
