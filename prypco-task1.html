Initiative 01: Funnel instrumentation.

You cannot optimize a funnel you cannot see. This ships before everything else.

What to track

Registration
Landing viewed · CTA tapped · Email entered · Signup completed
Wizard
Step 1 viewed · Step 2 viewed · Step 3 viewed · Property tapped
KYC
KYC started · ID scan · Selfie · Each step drop-off · Completed
Investment
Property viewed · Amount selected · Invest tapped · Payment done
Segments
UAE resident vs. international · Device · Acquisition channel

Why it must come first

  • Baseline for all 13 other initiatives

    Every initiative's impact is measured against this baseline. Without it, we cannot prove ROI.

  • Pinpoint the real drop-off step

    Current drop-off rates are directional estimates. Instrumentation tells us exactly where users leave.

  • Powers AI retargeting

    Initiative 12 (AI drop-off detection) depends entirely on real-time funnel event data.

  • Enables re-prioritization

    Once real data is available, effort vs impact scores are revisited and the roadmap adjusted.

Acquisition: hook & entry.

Capture all interested users before asking for anything.

02 · Value-led landing screen

The dream, not the product

  • Hero: 'Own a piece of Dubai real estate from AED 500' - speaks to aspiration, not features.
  • Real numbers front and centre. Projected ROI, gross yield, capital appreciation visible immediately.
  • Social proof above the fold: 3 real anonymized investor stories. VARA + Dubai Land Department badges.
  • One CTA: 'Start investing' - nothing else. No sign up, no friction before they're hooked.
03 · One-tap registration

Email or Google - nothing else

  • Zero forms, zero questions at the door. Name auto-populated from Google profile.
  • Email captured at step 1 - even if user bounces later, we can always re-engage.
  • No Emirates ID check at registration. All users enter the same funnel.
  • After one tap user goes straight into the investment wizard - not a KYC wall.

Together: 100% of interested users captured with re-engagement channel secured - before asking for any KYC information.

PRYPCORisks & Trade-offs
11 / 14

Risks, dependencies & trade-offs.

HIGH

Supply dependency

All investment initiatives fail without available properties. AI personalization on an empty shelf adds zero value. Fix: Marketplace optimization + listing cadence are prerequisites.

HIGH

VARA compliance

Rewards program, AI advisory, and international KYC need regulatory sign-off before any launch. Fix: Legal review in Sprint 1 before any incentive goes live.

MED

AI trust & explainability

KYC routing and investment recommendations must be auditable and non-discriminatory. Fix: Human override on all AI decisions. Full audit trail required.

MED

International KYC complexity

Passport-based verification for 180+ countries requires a global identity partner and expanded AML/sanctions coverage. Fix: Phase by low-risk nationality clusters first.

MED

Progressive KYC trade-off

Investing before full KYC may conflict with VARA's minimum verification requirements. Fix: Confirm minimum KYC threshold with VARA before launch.

LOW

Speed vs. quality

Rushing Sprint 1 without proper QA risks introducing new bugs into the critical onboarding path. Fix: Sprint 1 scoped narrowly - instrumentation + bugs + foundational flows only.
