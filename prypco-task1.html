Initiative 01: Funnel instrumentation.

You cannot optimize a funnel you cannot see. This ships before everything else.

What to track

Registration
Landing viewed · CTA tapped · Email entered · Signup completed
Wizard
Step 1 viewed · Step 2 viewed · Step 3 viewed · Property tapped
KYC
KYC started · ID scan · Selfie · Each step drop-off · Completed
Investment
Property viewed · Amount selected · Invest tapped · Payment done
Segments
UAE resident vs. international · Device · Acquisition channel

Recommended tools: Mixpanel or Amplitude · Sprint 1 · Owner: Engineering + Product

Why it must come first

  • Baseline for all 14 other initiatives

    Every initiative's impact is measured against this baseline. Without it, we cannot prove ROI.

  • Pinpoint the real drop-off step

    Current drop-off rates are directional estimates. Instrumentation tells us exactly where users leave.

  • Powers AI retargeting

    Initiative 12 (AI drop-off detection) depends entirely on real-time funnel event data to trigger re-engagement.

  • Enables ICE re-prioritization

    Once real data is available, ICE scores are revisited and the ICE roadmap adjusted accordingly.

PRYPCORisks & Trade-offs
9 / 12

Risks, dependencies & trade-offs.

HIGH

Supply dependency

All investment initiatives fail without available properties. AI personalization on an empty shelf adds zero value.

Fix: Marketplace optimization + listing cadence are prerequisites.
HIGH

VARA compliance

Incentives, AI advisory, and international KYC need regulatory sign-off before any launch.

Fix: Legal review in Sprint 1 before any incentive goes live.
MED

AI trust & explainability

KYC routing and investment recommendations must be auditable and non-discriminatory.

Fix: Human override on all AI decisions. Full audit trail required.
MED

Progressive KYC trade-off

Investing before full KYC may conflict with VARA's minimum verification requirements.

Fix: Phase by low-risk nationality clusters first, legal pre-clearance required.
